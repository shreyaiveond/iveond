About System Infrastructure

Transforming Business documents into business valueThe OCR technology converts virtually any kind of documents, such as scanned paper documents, PDF files, or images captured by a digital camera into machine-readable text data which is editable and searchable. It saves you a lot of time and effort when creating, processing, and repurposing various documents. We have developed this for various processes like Forms processing, Loan processing, Transcript processing, Invoice processing, etc.

  • Network Security
  • OCR software is used to convert handwritten, type-written or printed text into data that can be edited on a computer. In simple systems, the paper documents are scanned with an image scanner.
  • The OCR software then looks at the image and compares the shapes of the letters to stored images of letters. In this way, it makes a text file that can be edited with a normal text editor.

Achieve higher productivity with digitized documents with Optical Character Recognition .

Auto Document Classification

Our OCR Technology utilizes Artificial Intelligence (AI) to “read” documents like a human, to identify and classify the document into specific types and extract key data. These systems efficiently convert content from various sources into standard data types..

Auto Data Capture

This allows users to identify and extract data from forms and documents by detecting characters and fields on forms. With our expertise, you can now quickly and accurately process the documents.

Template Data Capture

The solution is designed to ensure data capture solutions with the capability to classify and recognize documents with different layouts at the same accuracy rates as that of structured forms.

Intelligent Data Capture (IDC)

Using our IDC solutions you get advanced AI capabilities like Machine Learning and NLP which allow scanning through documents to detect, analyze, and classify tables. This data is stored in a digital format for future business use.
